How long will my package take to arrive?

While we don't have a crystal ball to hand, our experience has taught us that on average the delivery times are as follows:

  • UK - 2-3 business days
  • Europe - 5-7 business days
  • Americas - 12-15 business days
  • Asia - 18-20 business days
  • Oceania - 14-16 business days

All of our products are packaged clearly for customs control, as we strive to get your items to you as quickly as possible. Customs can be notoriously difficult at times, though, so please be patient if your items haven't arrived by the time periods listed above.

Do you have a refund policy?

- We most certainly do. If there is an issue with your purchase, we will be happy to accommodate a replacement. Please note, however, that any items of under-clothing are non-refundable unless they arrived in a damaged state. When contacting us with your refund enquiry, please state the nature of the issue with the product thoroughly, as we maintain a high level of quality and care while the items are stored with us and would like to immediately highlight how and when the item could have gotten damaged.
